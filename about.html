About

Our mission:


Our goal is simple: to impact.

It's clear that diversity in the workplace is hugely integral to an employees success and a company's rate of retention. Without the intentional need to recruit from a diverse pool, we risk traditionally disadvantaged recruits to stay disadvantaged. Our platform provides you an opportunity to explain resume gaps, higher education decisions, and other circumstances to reintroduce humanity back into recruiting. Because these things do not produce "bad" candidates. It produces leaders.
